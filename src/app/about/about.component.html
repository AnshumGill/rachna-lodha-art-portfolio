Meet The Artist:
Rachna Lodha

Rachna Lodha is a graduate from Delhi University and completed Commercial Arts from New Delhi Polytechnic for Women. She has partaken in several group shows and workshops. Many of her artworks have been selected in All India Fine Art and Craft Society (AIFACS) exhibitions. Her artworks depict her endless trail of thoughts and the freedom of colours. She has been an artist by passion who breaks the boundaries and the current understanding that we have of the mediums used in art. Presently, she is practicing art in Delhi.
